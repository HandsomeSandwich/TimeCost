# TimeCost & Dinaro: A Behavioral Finance Ecosystem

**TimeCost** is a dual-purpose web application designed to bridge the gap between abstract monetary values and the concrete reality of "life-time" spent earning. It serves as both a high-fidelity personal finance calculator and a comprehensive family allowance/classroom management system called **Dinaro**.

---

## 1. The Core Philosophy: "Time as Currency"
The app's primary innovation is the **Time-to-Afford** logic. Instead of seeing a price tag of £1,000, users see the item in terms of "Work Days" or "Work Hours." This shifts the user's perspective from *can I afford this?* to *is this worth 3 days of my life?*

### Key Features:
*   **The Behavioral Calculator:** Users input their salary, work hours, and a target expense. The app calculates exactly how many hours of their life that item "costs."
*   **Contextual Comparisons:** It visualizes your earning power against world-famous billionaires, showing how many seconds it takes Elon Musk to afford your "big purchase" vs. how many days it takes you.

### Personality Toggles: The "Voice" of the App
The entire app’s tone, advice, and visual theme change based on three distinct "Perspectives," each inspired by iconic cultural archetypes. This turns a dry utility into a curated experience:

*   **Leslie (The Optimist):** Inspired by **Leslie Knope** (*Parks and Rec*). 
    *   *Tone:* High-energy, heroic, organized, and relentlessly positive. 
    *   *Marketing Angle:* Focuses on "The Power Plan" and "Future-You."
*   **Edie (The Realist):** Inspired by **Edina Monsoon** (*Absolutely Fabulous*). 
    *   *Tone:* Direct, cynical, fashion-forward, and brutally honest about the "ordeal" of spending. 
    *   *Marketing Angle:* Focuses on "The Dossier" and "Self-Respect" (with a glass of champagne).
*   **River (The Zen):** Inspired by **River Song** (*Doctor Who*). 
    *   *Tone:* Calm, minimal, mysterious, and focused on the "flow" of time and dimensions.
    *   *Marketing Angle:* Focuses on "The Timeline" and "Quiet Wealth."

---

## 2. The Dinaro System: Family & Classroom Economy
**Dinaro** is a modular "Economy-in-a-Box" built into the app. It allows parents or teachers to create a closed financial loop where children or students earn digital currency (Dinaro) through effort and spend it on real-world rewards.

### For Parents & Teachers:
*   **Chore/Task Management:** Create recurring jobs (Daily/Weekly) or one-time tasks with assigned "Time Values."
*   **Classroom Mode:** A dedicated toggle that shifts the terminology from "Parent/Child" to "Teacher/Student," making it suitable for educational environments.
*   **Wealth Trends:** A 7-day visual chart showing the financial progress and saving habits of every child/student.
*   **Automated Financials:** Features optional "Parent Taxes" (to teach subscriptions/expenses) and "Savings Bonuses" (to teach compound interest).

### For Children & Students:
*   **Adaptive Dashboards:** 
    *   **Visual Mode:** Large icons, emojis, and simplified "To-Do" lists for younger users.
    *   **Teen Mode:** Detailed ledger history, percentages, and complex goal tracking.
*   **The Marketplace:** A digital shop where kids can "Buy" rewards (like screen time, a toy, or a pizza party). They can even "Counter-Offer" a price to their parent.
*   **Gamification:** Users earn "Quirky Badges" (e.g., "Big Saver," "Master Worker") based on their financial behavior.

---

## 3. Technical & User Experience
*   **PWA (Progressive Web App):** The application is designed to be "installed" on a mobile phone home screen without using an App Store.
*   **Privacy-First Persistence:** Uses a lightweight PIN-based profile system. No email or social media login is required for children, keeping their data private and local to the "Family/Classroom" code.
*   **Universal Currency:** Supports £, $, €, ¥, ₹, ₩, and ₽ with real-time conversion logic for wealth comparisons.

---

**In summary:** TimeCost is more than a budgeter; it is a **behavioral education platform**. It teaches adults the value of their time and teaches children the mechanics of a modern economy through a fun, customizable, and safe digital environment.
